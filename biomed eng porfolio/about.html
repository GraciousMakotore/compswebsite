About

I design medical technologies that improve patient care. My work blends hardware prototyping, embedded sensing, and data analysis to create practical clinical tools used in healthcare settings.

My projects focus on three main areas: assistive devices that restore or augment function, patient monitoring systems that provide reliable physiological data, and medical imaging workflows that improve image acquisition and analysis. I prototype hardware, integrate sensors and embedded controllers, and build analysis tools to evaluate performance.

I emphasize safety, usability, and measurable clinical impact. That means early engagement with clinicians and users, rapid iteration with low-fidelity prototypes, and careful validation against real-world constraints. I also document designs to support reproducibility and future regulatory steps when appropriate.

Core skills

  • Medical imaging & signal processing
  • Embedded systems & sensor integration
  • Prototyping & rapid fabrication
  • Data analysis (Python, MATLAB)
